mpn_gcdext computes: (G,S) = gcdext(U,V), where

  • G = gcd(U,V)
  • G = U*S + V*T
  • U has equal or more limbs than V, and V has no padding If we set U to be (a padded version of) a, and V = m: G = a*S + m*T G = a*S mod m Assuming G=1: S = 1/a mod m
